Customer Care


At Trevz Chicken Farm, our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional customer service, ensuring that every interaction you have with us is positive and fulfilling. From the moment you visit our website or farm store, our dedicated team is here to assist you with any questions or concerns. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, founded on trust, transparency, and a shared passion for quality organic products. Whether you're placing an order, inquiring about our farming practices, or seeking advice on how to best enjoy our products, we're always here to help.


We understand that our commitment to excellence extends beyond the farm. That's why we go the extra mile to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. Our customer care team is readily available to address any issues, provide personalized recommendations, and ensure timely delivery of your orders.




Privacy & Safety


At Trevz Chicken Farm, the safety of our customers and our products is our top priority. We adhere to the highest standards of food safety and hygiene throughout every stage of our farming and production processes. Our facilities are regularly inspected, and our staff is trained to follow strict safety protocols to ensure that our poultry and eggs are always fresh, clean, and safe for your family to enjoy.


Your privacy is of utmost importance to us at Trevz Chicken Farm. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and ensuring that it is handled with the highest level of security and confidentiality. When you interact with us—whether through our website, farm store, or customer service—we use state-of-the-art security measures to safeguard your data.
